Data Protection and Privacy Law (Ethiopian Context)

Overview of data protection, privacy principles, and cybersecurity-related laws with emphasis on Ethiopian legal and regulatory frameworks.

About This Program

This course introduces learners to key concepts of data protection and privacy, focusing on Ethiopian legislation and compliance requirements. Participants learn to interpret laws, implement policies, and manage personal and sensitive data responsibly in organizations.

Who Should Attend

This program is designed for:

  • Legal professionals
  • Government officers
  • Data protection officers
  • Managers

Learning Outcomes

Understand data protection principles
Interpret Ethiopian cyber and data laws
Apply compliance measures in organizations

Requirements

  • Basic understanding of organizational data handling
  • Interest in privacy and cybersecurity laws

Certification

Certificate of Completion upon ≥75% attendance and passing the final written assessment

Upon successful completion of the program and all required assessments

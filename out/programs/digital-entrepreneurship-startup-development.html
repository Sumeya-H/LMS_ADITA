From Idea to Launch: Digital Entrepreneurship and Startup Development

A foundational course guiding aspiring entrepreneurs from idea conception to a validated business model and MVP launch plan.

About This Program

This course provides a comprehensive roadmap for launching a digital venture. Participants move from a raw idea to a validated business model, learning customer discovery, market analysis, and lean startup methodology. Emphasis is placed on the African digital market, enabling entrepreneurs to build innovative and viable ventures.

Who Should Attend

This program is designed for:

  • Aspiring entrepreneurs
  • Students
  • Intrapreneurs
  • Anyone with a digital business idea

Learning Outcomes

Apply the Lean Canvas to refine a business idea
Conduct customer discovery and market validation
Define and prototype a Minimum Viable Product (MVP)
Understand startup legal and financial considerations
Develop a phased launch plan for a digital venture

Requirements

  • Basic computer literacy
  • Interest in entrepreneurship
  • No prior business experience required

Certification

Certificate of Completion in Digital Entrepreneurship and Startup Development

Upon successful completion of the program and all required assessments
